# Medical Imaging & Data Science Journey 🧬📊

Welcome! I am a **B.Sc. Medical Radiology and Imaging Technology** student (currently in my 3rd year) working at the intersection of clinical radiology and advanced technology. 

My ultimate goal upon graduation is to become a **Data Scientist specializing in Medical AI and Computer Vision**, bridging the gap between healthcare data and intelligent software.

---

## 🎯 My Mission & Focus

Instead of just analyzing medical images clinically, I am learning to build the algorithms that help interpret them. This repository serves as my digital notebook and portfolio as I develop skills to:

*   **Process Medical Data:** Work with digital imaging data (like X-rays, CTs, and MRIs) at a pixel and voxel level using code.
*   **Apply Computer Vision:** Learn how computers analyze patterns, detect anomalies, and segment anatomical structures.
*   **Develop AI Tools:** Build machine learning models capable of assisting radiologists and improving diagnostic workflows.

---

## 🛠️ The Toolkit I am Building

*   **Domain Expertise:** Anatomy, Radiology Physics, Contrast Mechanisms, CT/MRI/X-Ray Modalities.
*   **Programming & Core Data Science:** Python, NumPy (numerical arrays), Pandas (data analysis).
*   **Image Processing & AI:** OpenCV (computer vision), PyDicom (medical image handling), Matplotlib (data visualization).
